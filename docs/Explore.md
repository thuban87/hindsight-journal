# Explore

The Explore tab group gives you tools to search, browse, and discover across your entire journal.

---

## Lens

Full-text and frontmatter search across all indexed entries.

### How it works
- Type a query to search across entry content and section headings
- Results show matching entries with highlighted terms and excerpts
- Supports searching within specific sections if you want to narrow it down

### Filters
Use frontmatter filters to find entries where specific fields match certain values. Combine text search with field filters for precise queries, like "entries where mood > 7 that mention 'workout'."

---

## Threads

Tag-based exploration of your journal.

### Tag frequency chart
A bar chart showing how often each tag appears across your entries. Click a bar to filter and see all entries with that tag.

### Cross-tag exploration
Select a tag to see what other tags commonly appear alongside it. This helps you find recurring themes and connections between topics.

---

## Gallery

![Gallery view](../assets/images/Hindsight%20Gallery_DM.png)

A visual grid of all images embedded in your journal entries.

### Features
- Thumbnail grid with dates shown below each image
- Click any thumbnail to open a full-size lightbox view
- Images are pulled from all supported formats: wiki-links (`![[image.png]]`), markdown embeds (`![alt](image.png)`), and linked images
- Thumbnail generation runs in the background and caches results in IndexedDB for fast loading
