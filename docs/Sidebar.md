# Sidebar

The sidebar lives in Obsidian's right panel and gives you a quick at-a-glance view without opening the full journal interface.

![Sidebar with Today panel](../assets/images/Hindsight%20Sidebar_DM.png)

---

## Today

The Today tab is your daily dashboard. It includes several configurable widgets:

### Entry status
Shows whether today's entry exists, how many frontmatter fields are filled in, your current writing streak, and when the entry was last edited. Two buttons let you **open today's note** or **quick-edit** frontmatter values without leaving the sidebar.

### Goal progress
Circular progress rings for each field you've set a weekly goal on. Shows completion as a percentage with the fraction (e.g., "26/49 weekly").

### Sparklines
Mini trend charts for each detected numeric field, showing the last several values at a glance. A quick way to see if a metric is trending up or down.

### Gap alerts
Flags fields that have missing data in recent entries. For example, "studying_hours_done has gaps: 6 of last 7 days missing." Helps you stay on top of fields you might be forgetting to fill in.

### Morning briefing
Pulls in context from your recent journal to start the day. Includes your writing streak and quick stats.

### Layout customization
Click **Edit layout** at the bottom to show reorder arrows and visibility toggles for each widget. Move widgets up or down, hide ones you don't need, and click **Done** when you're finished. Your layout preferences are saved automatically.

## Echoes

![Echoes panel](../assets/images/Hindsight%20Echoes_DM.png)

The Echoes tab surfaces entries from your past so you can reflect on where you've been.

### Controls
- **Period** selector: "On this day," "This week in past years," or "This month in past years"
- **Section** filter: pick a specific heading section to compare (e.g., "Dreams," "Top 3")
- **Metric** selector: choose a frontmatter field to display on echo cards

### Echo cards
Each card shows the date, an excerpt from the entry (or from the selected section), a metric badge, and the word count. Click a card to open that entry.

### Additional features
- **Metric comparison cards** showing how a field's value on a past date compares to your current average
- **Coping strategy lookup** to surface what worked in the past during similar periods
- **Milestone markers** for notable dates
